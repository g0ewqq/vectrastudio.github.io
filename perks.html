Perks

Thank you to everyone who boosts the Vectra Studios Discord. Boosts cover hosting and tooling costs, and unlock the perks below as a thank-you.

What you get

Six perks for boosters

These are the perks boosters unlock when they support the server.

Booster Badge

A permanent badge on your profile and in Discord, recognising your support.

Exclusive Downloads

Access to premium resources and plugins not available publicly.

Premium Resources

Curated configs, setup templates and tools shared with boosters first.

Early Access Projects

Try new plugins and setups before public release, with feedback channels.

Priority Support

Faster response times on support tickets and commission quotes.

Exclusive Channels

Private Discord channels for boosters to discuss and request features.

How it works

Three steps to unlock

Step 01

Boost the server on Discord

Open the Vectra Studios Discord and boost the server from the server menu. Any boost level counts.

Step 02

Role synced automatically

Discord role synchronization is being wired up — perks will activate automatically once linked.

Step 03

Perks unlock instantly

Once your boost is active, all the perks listed above are yours.

A note on role sync

Booster perks are managed through Discord. Boost the server and your perks activate automatically.

Boosts keep the studio's open-source work shipping and the community Discord running. Every boost counts.
